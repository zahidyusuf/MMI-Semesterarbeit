Our project

Our goal

  • End the use of steel-jaw leghold traps and reform other brutal methods
    of capturing and killing wildlife

  • Preserve species threatened with extinction, and protect wildlife
    from harmful exploitation and destruction of critical habitat

  • Protect companion animals from cruelty and violence,
    including appalling conditions in commercial trade

Our story

Our organisation was created in 1994, following the vision of
its founder, John Doe. We took to ourselves to travel all around
the world and save as many animals as possible, especially those
in immediate danger. Since then, we have raised around 56.000.000 € and saved
thousands of animals, keep the much needed environmental balance our planet needs.
